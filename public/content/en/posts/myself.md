This year I was born — May 31st. First time opening my eyes.

## 2014

Two main things this year. First, I started elementary school, and from this point on I was a boarding student. Second, I discovered Minecraft. This game later became the most important game in my life. No contest.

## 2015

Think it was this year — mobile games started exploding in popularity. I jumped on the mobile gaming train for the first time. By the end of the year, I was playing CF (CrossFire).

## 2016

Had a great time playing with friends this year. Too bad I was the only one in class who played Minecraft, but they all played CF with me. All in all, great memories.

## 2017

This year I got my first computer and started learning Python. Since Minecraft felt better on PC, I gradually shifted to prioritizing the computer. Towards the end of the year, the anti-addiction verification caught up with me — I couldn't keep playing CF anymore, so from then on it was Minecraft all the way.
