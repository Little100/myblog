## Overview

This policy explains what information this personal blog may collect when you visit, how it is used, and your choices. The site is operated as a static-first project; there is no user account system.

## Information we may collect

Server and hosting providers may automatically log technical data such as IP address, browser type, and request time when you load pages.

If you use the contact email link, you choose what you send; we only use it to respond to your message.

## How we use information

We use data to operate and secure the site, understand aggregate traffic patterns, and reply to messages you send voluntarily.

We do not sell your personal information.

## Third-party services

Embedded features (for example comment widgets or analytics, if enabled in configuration) are provided by their respective operators under their own policies.

Review those services if you interact with them on this site.

## Cookies and storage

The site may use browser storage to remember preferences such as theme or language.

You can clear site data in your browser settings at any time.

## Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold.

Contact us using the email on the contact page and we will respond within a reasonable time.

## Questions

For privacy-related questions, please use the contact page. This policy may be updated occasionally; the latest version will always be published here.
